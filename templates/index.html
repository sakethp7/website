About Aavishkaran E-Cell

Aavishkaran E-Cell at our college is a platform dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship among students. We provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities for aspiring entrepreneurs to turn their ideas into reality. Join us in shaping the future of entrepreneurship!
FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is Aavishkaran E-Cell? Aavishkaran E-Cell is the entrepreneurship cell of our college dedicated to fostering entrepreneurial spirit among students.
How can I get involved with Aavishkaran E-Cell? You can get involved by participating in our events, workshops, and competitions or by joining as a member of the E-Cell team.
What kind of events does Aavishkaran E-Cell organize? Aavishkaran E-Cell organizes a variety of events including startup pitch competitions, guest lectures, networking sessions, and hackathons.
How can I stay updated about upcoming events? You can stay updated about upcoming events by regularly visiting our website or following us on social media platforms for announcements.
Can non-business students participate in Aavishkaran E-Cell activities? Yes, Aavishkaran E-Cell welcomes participation from students of all disciplines who are interested in entrepreneurship and innovation.
